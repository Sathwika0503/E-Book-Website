ABOUT THIS SITE


Hello!

Nowadays the internet is a part of our routine, and we search for almost everything online. Given the fast-paced lives these days, we would want everything at a clicks distance. Therefore, people are preferring ebooks instead of going to libraries as it saves our time. There are many websites which provide ebooks on different topics, but it takes more time to search for ebooks related to our subjects. In order to optimize this process we aim to design and engineer a web based application which would serve as a one-stop-solution to find a referral link to books that are a part of the IT curriculum at MVSR.
